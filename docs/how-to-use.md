# How To Use Skipisode

1. Open a YouTube watch page.
2. Find the circular `Skip` button on the page.
3. Click the `Skip` button to open the control panel.
4. Play the video until the intro ends.
5. Click `Set intro end` to save the intro skip point.
6. Replay the video or open another matching episode.
7. Let the extension skip the intro automatically.
8. Play the video until the outro or credits begin.
9. Click `Set outro start` to save the outro skip point.
10. Replay the video or open another matching episode.
11. Let the extension skip the outro automatically near the end.
12. If the current watch page belongs to a playlist, use `Apply to playlist` after saving a rule.
13. Open another video from the same playlist.
14. Confirm the playlist rule is applied automatically.
15. Drag the floating `Skip` button if you want to move it to a different position.
16. Refresh the page and confirm the button stays where you placed it.
